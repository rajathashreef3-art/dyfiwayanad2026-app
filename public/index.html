📋 How to install on Android via Chrome

1

Open this page in Chrome

Make sure you are using Google Chrome on Android. If you are in another browser, copy the link and paste it into Chrome.

🌐
Chrome — the colourful circle icon on your phone
2

Tap "Open App in Chrome" above

The conference web app opens in your Chrome browser tab. You will see the DYFI login and registration page.

👆
Tap the green button above to open the app
3

Tap the 3-dot menu ⋮ in Chrome

In Chrome, tap the three dots ⋮ at the top-right corner to open the browser menu.

⋮
3 dots at top-right corner of Chrome browser
4

Tap "Add to Home screen"

Scroll in the menu and tap "Add to Home screen". You may also see a pop-up banner at the bottom of Chrome — tap that instead.

🏠
"Add to Home screen" in Chrome's ⋮ menu
5

Tap "Install" to confirm

A confirmation popup appears. Tap "Install". The DYFI app icon will appear on your Android home screen immediately!

✅
Install → App icon appears on home screen
💡 Once installed, the app opens full-screen like a native app — no browser toolbar. Works even with slow internet!
🍎

Install via Safari (iPhone / iPad)

Add to home screen · iOS web app · No App Store needed

✓ iPhone Compatible iOS 14+ · Safari
🧭 Open App in Safari

📋 How to install on iPhone via Safari

1

Open this page in Safari

You must use Safari on iPhone — this feature does NOT work in Chrome or Firefox on iOS. Safari is the blue compass icon on your home screen.

🧭
Safari — blue compass icon on your iPhone
2

Tap "Open App in Safari" above

Tap the blue button above. The DYFI conference app will open in Safari browser.

👆
Tap the blue button above to open the app
3

Tap the Share button ⬆️

At the bottom toolbar of Safari, tap the Share button — it looks like a square with an upward arrow ↑ pointing out of it.

⬆️
Share button at the bottom bar of Safari
4

Scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen"

The share sheet slides up. Scroll down in the list of options until you see "Add to Home Screen" (with a square-plus icon). Tap it.

🏠
"Add to Home Screen" — scroll down in share sheet
5

Tap "Add" in the top-right corner

A screen shows the app name. Tap "Add" in the top-right. The DYFI app icon will appear on your iPhone home screen instantly!

✅
Tap Add → App icon on iPhone home screen
💡 After adding, the app opens in full-screen mode on your iPhone — no Safari address bar, just like a real App Store app!
